Hello!

I'm a Michigan gal, currently soaking up the sweet, slow life near Lake Michigan, a far cry from my former Chicago hustle. Let's just say, trading traffic jams for sunset strolls has been a major upgrade.

My life's been a bit of a scenic route, if you will. I started with a degree in graphic design, because, you know, visuals! Then, because I apparently like collecting degrees like shiny Pokémon, I grabbed another in English. Why? Because words are magic! And now, I'm on a mission to conquer the library world with a Master’s in Library Science. Yep, a lifelong library lover finally taking the plunge.

Present and Future

Currently, I'm wrangling data as a senior analyst for a home improvement retailer. Think of me as a digital detective, but instead of solving crimes, I'm figuring out what kind of drills people like. Before that, I dabbled in manufacturing, which was…an experience. Let's just say I learned the true meaning of "industrial strength."

My grand plan? To become a librarian in my "golden" years. Because who wouldn't want to spend their days surrounded by books? And maybe, just maybe, I’ll dabble in informational architecture, because organizing information is a superpower I'd love to refine.

I’m a digital native, folks. Online learning? Been there, done that, got the virtual diploma(s). I’m a big fan of learning on-demand, because who has time to wait?
