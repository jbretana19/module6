Saint Joseph, Michigan

Saint Joseph, Michigan, is a great place to unwind. You can picture yourself taking in the beautiful Lake Michigan views from Silver Beach, and those sunsets are something else-- especially when viewed from the north pier of the local lighthouse. Downtown has wonderful local shops and restaurants and is perfect for a stroll along the bluff that overlooks Lake Michigan. There's much to do, from nature-filled walks to taking a ride on a historic carousel. Basically, St. Joe offers a relaxed atmosphere with plenty to see and do, making it a solid choice for any vacation!

A Notable Michigan Attraction

Pictured Rocks

Pictured Rocks National Lakeshore is a stunning place on Lake Superior. The cliffs are its most famous feature, with colorful streaks from minerals. You'll find beaches, waterfalls, and sand dunes too. There are many ways to explore, like hiking, kayaking, or boat tours. In winter, you can snowshoe or ski. It's a beautiful area with a lot to see.
